UNLIT

Unlit is a project that I come back to regularly, and not release. It is created with very talented Gökhan Çınar link.

The ground concept of Unlit is the product of meditation on the touch screen controls and how to overcome their inherent suckiness. This "control-centric" design philosophy created strict boundaries.

Designated area at the lower half of the screen is reserved for controls.
• 9 pieces of the area corresponds to the 9 points on the game board which character snaps to.
• Player controls the character by moving their finger on the control area.
• Finger position (almost) instantly reflects to the character position while respecting the snapping.

This direct projection of finger to character movement and instantaneous game feedback creates highly dynamic gameplay opportunities.

PLANET RUNNERS

Mobile multiplayer racing game; which is heavily influenced by endless runner games, SpeedRunners and MOBAs. I co-created Planet Runners with Burç Tuncer link. Burç created all the visuals alongside with amazing soundtrack which you should definitely check out.

We aimed to create this game with "ULTRA MEGA SIMPLE CONTROLS" nevertheless with a strong sense of control and fluidity. Heroes have different stats and various skills to overcome opponents.

Early in design, we decided not to use any directional input. Players need to use basic controls (jump, dash) at the right time to indirectly control characters. This required a lot of tuning and balancing, meanwhile having an online multiplayer layer was a challenge.

The absence of directional input created quite interesting conditions. For example, creating cyclic levels. We tried to overcome this with arrow elements that change the direction of the character and correctly placed springs.

EVRENLER ARASI MACERA

"One day, a giant star falls into the neighborhood! This confuses the residents and causes them to lose their way to home! A child finally realizes that the star which hit to the neighborhood that day was the North Star, known as the guide to find one’s direction."

Created with ROOT link for the Save The Children link, Evrenler Arası Macera aims to raise awareness about peer bullying.
Players create their avatars with modular skin parts. Two teams are created with one or more players, they answer questions through 4 different themed landscapes featuring beautiful artwork and soundtrack.

Teams proceed through a path. Most path nodes have a defined area which corresponds to the 2D background.
Game board consists of many animated cute assets that need to be organized to layers and dynamically sorted with passing players.

We created various editor tools to visualize and automatize creation and iteration of design elements. Paths and boundaries, corresponding animations and sounds was subject to change during development regarding to customer feedback. Having reliable editor extensions were essential.

VOODOO MAMBO

Being one of my first released games as a developer, this project holds relevancy. In this game, player uses its two fingers to merge balls on the touch screen with a pinch gesture.
It was essential to catch a feeling of ease at the pinch gesture. While gameplay required some motor skills, keeping the interference of basic physical failures to minimum was a clear aim we chased as a team. In retrospect, I believe we successfully realized the potential of the defined scope.
